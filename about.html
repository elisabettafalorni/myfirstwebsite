This website was created thanks to a class of Informatics, named Informatica Umanistica, of the University of Bologna.
I chose to explore the theme of the numerous links existing between two fascinating art forms to increase my knowledge and share it with everyone who is interested. The purpose is to make people understand that the different art forms are not completely different worlds: the inspiration can come from anything, especially if, as Kandinskij was, the artist is capable of synesthesia.
