QUEEN


If you are wondering why Queen are included in this list, here is your answer.
Innuendo is a song contained in the homonym album that shows in a simple and clear way the connection between music and some of the most famous art works.
The reference to art is in this videoclip and not in the lyrics, indeed every member of the band is represented like a cinema projection in a different painting style.
The guitarist Brian May is represented with the typical features of Pollock's style, Freddy Mercury seems painted by Leonardo da Vinci, the bassist John Deacon represents Cubism of Picasso, the Roger Taylor is an abstract painting by Kandinskij.
Watching the video we can also find objects like mannequins and masks that are referring to the twentieth century's avant-gardes like Futurism and De Chiricoâ€™s metaphysical paintings.
